## App Description

### Short Description (for search/preview)

### Full Description

**Wind down. Sleep better.**

Night Routine is a beautifully simple app designed to help you build a peaceful evening routine. No complicated features, no social pressure—just a calm checklist that helps you end each day with intention.

**Why Night Routine?**

We believe the secret to better sleep starts before your head hits the pillow. Night Routine gives you a gentle, judgment-free way to build healthy evening habits that prepare your mind and body for rest.

**Features:**

• **Simple Checklist** — Tap to complete each step. That's it. No complicated workflows.

• **Customizable Routine** — Create your own wind-down steps or use our thoughtful defaults: skincare, brush teeth, set phone down, stretch, water, lights off.

• **Gentle Streaks** — Track your consistency without shame. Miss a night? No guilt, no lectures. Tomorrow's a new day.

• **Skip Without Guilt** — Not feeling it tonight? Tap "Not tonight—and that's okay" for a compassionate exit.

• **Nightly Reminders** — A gentle notification at your chosen time to start winding down.

• **Quiet Mode** — Dims the screen and disables haptics for a calmer experience before bed.

• **Beautiful & Dark** — Designed with your nighttime eyes in mind. Dark theme by default, calming purple accents.

• **Completely Private** — All your data stays on your device. We don't collect anything.

**Premium (One-time Purchase):**
• Unlimited routine steps
• Custom reminder messages
• Quote theme packs (Calm, Romantic, Stoic, Minimal, Encouraging)

Build better nights. Wake up feeling ready.
