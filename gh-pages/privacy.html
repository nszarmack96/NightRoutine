Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Summary: Night Routine does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your routine data stays on your device.

Introduction

Night Routine ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Night Routine is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All routine data is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage
  • We do not have servers that receive your data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal information

Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your routine steps and customizations
  • Completion history and streak data
  • App settings (reminder time, quiet mode preferences)
  • Premium purchase status (verified through Apple)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are purchased through Apple's App Store. We do not process or store any payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple according to their Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable reminder notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification servers and cannot see when or if you receive notifications.

Third-Party Services

Night Routine does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Night Routine does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Deleting the Night Routine app from your device
  • Using the "Reset to Defaults" option within the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
